The 6 rules — break these and the plan collapses
25
Pomodoro only
25 min study. 5 min break. Never go longer. The timer is not optional — it's the whole system.
1
One topic per session
CSCP one block, AWS the next. Never mix them in the same 25 minutes. Context switching destroys retention.
2×
Max sessions per day
Two Pomodoros on weekday evenings. More = burnout and quitting. Small and consistent beats marathon sessions every time.
3×
Recall after every session
Close everything. Write 3 things you remember without looking. This doubles retention. Do not skip.
FRI
Review day — no new material
Fridays are for Anki flashcards and practice questions only. Reviewing what you learned beats learning something new.
SUN
10-min weekly plan
Every Sunday: check what you completed, set focus for next week. Replaces anxiety with clarity.
⚠️ The #1 ADHD study killer: Doing nothing all week then studying 3 hours on Saturday. It feels productive. It builds nothing. Two focused evenings beat one marathon session every single time.
✓ The cheat code: Tell ONE person about this plan. Accountability increases follow-through by ~33% — that's not motivational fluff, that's research. Text a friend today.
Low-energy days are real. On those days: watch a 5-minute YouTube video about AWS or supply chain instead of doing nothing. Keeping the streak alive matters more than intensity.

After each session — write these 3 things
1
What was the main topic?
Name it in one sentence without looking at your notes.
2
What surprised you?
Anything you didn't know or that was different from what you expected.
3
What was confusing?
Flag it. Search it next session or add it to Anki as a question.
